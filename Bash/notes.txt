Change to home directory (~):
	cd

Manual pages:
	man ls
		q (exit)
		b (back)
		space (down)
		/use a long listing (search)

Print working directory:
	pwd

Hidden files start with a full stop, to see them:
	ls -a

Special files:
	. (current directory)
	.. (parent directory)

History:
	Use up and down cursors, or Ctrl-p and Ctrl-n.
